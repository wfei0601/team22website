Team Objectives


The first goal our team came to a consensus on was to get better at storytelling. This was the most glaring issue our team has identified following the EY Case Competition. We think learning this skill will be vital for all of the presentations we will give this semester, and it needs to be accomplished before we truly be successful in our case analysis. After we accomplish our storytelling goal, we would like to start working on our presentation skills. This is the next step on our team’s journey to Next, in November we would like to turn our focus onto our relationships within the team. This will be the culmination of all of the goals we have outlined above, and also our efforts both in and outside the classroom.
