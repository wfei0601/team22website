Performance Evaluation Criteria


The performance evaluation criteria includes the overall outcome of a project, the quality of work contributed, and productivity level. We choose grades and outcome of the project because it is important to all of the team member's performance in the core. We chose quality of work flow because we want all of our team members to contribute its maximum effort to each assignment. Finally, we factor in the time management aspect of the project because we all have a lot of things going on both in MSIS and in our personal lives, and we want to be efficient with our time spent working on projects outside of the classroom.
